SUNNICORN5678'S Roblox Review

Roblox is an app that allows users to play a wide variety of games, create games, and chat with others online. It combines gaming, social media, and social commerce. Billing itself as the “ultimate virtual universe,” Roblox experiences are places where users can socialize, build their own spaces, and even earn and spend virtual money. Games on Roblox are officially called “experiences” which fall into a variety of genres like roleplay, adventure, fighting, obby (obstacle courses), tycoon, simulator, and more.
